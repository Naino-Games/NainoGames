Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Naino Games, accessible from this website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Naino Games and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We currently do not require you to create an account to play our games.

  • Log Files: Like many other websites, Naino Games uses log files. These files merely log visitors to the site - a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to store your game progress locally on your device (e.g., high scores in Flappy Nain, unlocked levels in Naino Drive). These are strictly necessary for game functionality and are not sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and games
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website (e.g., which games are most popular)
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Naino Games' Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.

4. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Naino Games does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at nainogame@gmail.com.
